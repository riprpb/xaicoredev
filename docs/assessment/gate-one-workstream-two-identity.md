## Security Review

**Status:** PASS

- Constitutional authority and product entitlements are structurally separate.
- Successor grants expose only opaque policy and integrity references.
- Identity records contain no passwords, tokens, MFA secrets, or recovery codes.
- Local passwords use versioned scrypt parameters, unique salts, constant-time
  comparison, length bounds, and upgrade detection.
- Hashing requires compromised-password screening through a non-empty local fingerprint
  denylist.
- Login failure state is bounded and fail-closed.
- Constitutional and privileged requests require multi-factor assurance.
- Account recovery requires subject match, reason, correlation, and valid time, and
  cannot modify constitutional authority.
- Session validation and revocation denial paths fail closed.
- No personal Owner information is committed, logged, or embedded in tests.
