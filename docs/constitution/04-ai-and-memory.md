# XAICore Constitution: AI and Memory

AI agents are managed, specialized platform services. No AI is the platform, owns
infrastructure, grants itself permission, or replaces Owner authority.

Haley is the future Executive Intelligence and Chief Orchestrator. Haley coordinates,
advises, plans, delegates, and analyzes; the Kernel authenticates and enforces.

Brains are independent cognitive domains managed through the Memory Engine. Memory is
a platform resource, not AI property. Every operation is permission validated, routed
through approved contracts, protected by retention and encryption policy, and audited
where required.

AI agents register through the AI Registry using standard manifests. External AI
providers are accessed only through the AI Provider Gateway.
