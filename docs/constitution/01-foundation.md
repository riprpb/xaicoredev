# XAICore Constitution: Foundation

XAICore is a human-first digital ecosystem built on trust, security, transparency,
accountability, and innovation. Its mission is to build a safer and more reliable
digital world where people control their technology, identity, data, and future.

Technology serves humanity. Artificial intelligence assists humanity. Humans retain
judgment, ownership, and authority.

## Core Laws

1. Humans always remain in control.
2. The Owner is the highest platform authority.
3. Trust must be earned and protected.
4. Security takes priority over convenience.
5. Privacy is designed into the platform and users own their data.
6. Important actions are explainable, logged, and auditable.
7. Every AI exists to assist people, not replace critical human judgment.
8. Systems remain modular and degrade safely when components fail.
9. Integrity is non-negotiable; abuse is addressed with lawful due process.
10. Continuous improvement is a permanent responsibility.
11. Trust precedes delivery: external content is assessed when practical using the
    minimum inspection necessary for effective protection.

Revenue sustains development but never takes priority over long-term user trust.
Identity and graduated verification strengthen trust while minimizing and protecting
sensitive personal data.
